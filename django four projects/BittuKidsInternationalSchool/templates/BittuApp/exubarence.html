Exuberance



The Annual Extravaganza ‘EXUBERANCE 2018’ of the Primary Wing of Delhi Public School. Rasra was held on Saturday, 1st December, 2018 at ‘RIVERA’ the school auditorium. The programme commenced with the gracious presence of the Chairman Superhouse Group Mr. Mukhtarul Amin, Vice Chairperson, Mrs. Shahina Amin, Director (S.E.F.) and Principal of the school,Mrs. Rinku yadav.The evening preceded with the blessings of lord Ganesha, presented through the ‘Vandana’. The scintillating dances, the fantabulous choir and the fillers kept the audience mesmerized and amused. The auditorium echoed with loud cheers and applause after every performance . The main attractions of the evening were ‘Rang Rangeelo Rajasthan’, The UV light show ‘Bounce n Bubble’ and ‘Mirage De Sept’. The ‘Save the Tiger’ project was very effectively portrayed by the little buddies in the item,‘Magnificence Entrapped’ – Tears of Tiger.The programme ended on a positive note with a vote of thanks by the Principal, Mrs. Rinku yadav
