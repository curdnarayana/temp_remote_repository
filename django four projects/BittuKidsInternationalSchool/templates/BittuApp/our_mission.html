Our Academic Vision & Mission



“We at Bittu kids public school Rasra-Ballia, find success in each one, some way, every day”. Our child centered philosophy has led us to a deeper commitment to enhancing the learning process in light of increased understanding of how children learn. The aim is to lead the students from the front by the teachers becoming role models and promoting and encouraging them in all the vistas of talent. Our emphasis on addressing individual needs of the child leads to continuous recognition, validation of our dedication to children & to making modifications which enhance the learning environment. The school believes in the adage ‘service before self’ and this is a reflection of the inner spirit and essence of the working of the school. To meet a long-felt need of providing quality educational facilities to our younger children, Bittu kids public school Rasra-Ballia has been opened. The School is located 8kms. away from Rasra or 45kms. away from Ballia on Ballia-Lucknow State Highway. The School was started on jul., 2, 2012. It is an English medium institution co-educational and prepares students for CBSE examination. This institution aims at all round physical, mental moral and spiritual development of the child. Special care is taken to make the child a useful member of the society.
