Welcome to Bittu Kids Public School



Bittu Kids Public School was founded on the 2nd july 2012 with the prime aim of imparting a comprehensive modern education to the children with valuable Indian tradition of education in Patna & it's surroundings irrespective of caste, creed and community. The institute is located in plush premises at Ballia. It is 45 km away from Ballia Railway, Bus station surrounded by attractive healthy, natural environment that creates favourable academic conditions for alround development of children. Since the day of inception it has been determined to scale an unparalleled new graph of success to impart it to the children. We want our children to imbibe moral values in life. At present the rapid changes in technology and accelerated pace of privatisation and liberalisation created a new sense of uncertainity about the future of society. Thus education should respond meaningfully and effectively to address the crucial academic burning issues. Bittu Kids Public School in its efforts is providing need-based quality education to the blossoming knowledge which has decided to equip the children will pragmatic skills so that they can face the future with renewed confidence and matchless courage. The president of school is always nurturing the institute keeping sight and presenting all the facilities with help of management committee.
