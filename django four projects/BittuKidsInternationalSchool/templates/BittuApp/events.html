Events of Panorama 2019



Arts

P01 COLOR SPLASH - CREATE YOUR STYLE

No. of participants - 2 Time - 3 hours Theme - Contemporary Expression. The host school will provide a White Chart paper. Participants are ALLOWED TO USE ANY MEDIUM (Poster Colours, Acrylic etc.) of their choice. Your composition can be imaginary and contemporary according to your own perception.



P02 BANNER MOTIF (BANNER DESIGNING)

No. of participants - 2. Time - 3 hours Theme:- (a) Success through education (b) Technology Addiction The host school will provide a BANNER (Flexible Vinyl) of size 5’ x 2’ – Vertical long. Participants are required to bring paints (Acrylic, Permanent Pens etc.) to incorporate design and theme.



P03 ECO BAG DESIGNING

No. of participants - 2. Time - 2 hours The participants can use their creativity and skills to make an ecofriendly Bag. It should be sturdy of size 14” x 10” inches. Participants have to bring requisites. (Magazine, Newspaper, Craft Paper, Card board, other recycle material for decoration, fevicol etc.). Semi-made bags of pre – prepared materials is not allowed.

P04 GAMER’S HELL

No. of participants :1 Modes of Gaming: PS3, PS4/PC Usage of cheat codes will lead to disqualification



P05 TECHNOTOON

No. of participants : 2 The participants are expected to create an animation story for 3 - 4 minutes using the provided Softwares: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Premier Pro CS6, FL Studio 11, Macromedia, Flash, Filmora 8.7, Camtasia STUDIO 8.



P06 CRAZY CODER

No. of participant : 1 First round qualifying round Top 6 teams will appear for final round. Coding on C++/ JAVA.



CULTURAL

P07 RANGMANCH

Minimum 5 participants. Performing Theme: Folk dance of any 1 state. Time Limit: 5 - 7 minutes including Entry & Exit. (Exceeding the time limit will result in deduction of points) Stage Size: 15 x 10 ft. School team will bring only pen drive in audio format no CDs will be accepted. Submit your song at the time of registration i.e., at the registration counter.



P08 NUKKAD NATAK

A minimum of 4 and a maximum of 6 participants are allowed. Time limit : 5 - 8 minutes Language: Hindi/Hinglish. The play should be original. The Schools which require props (banners with slogans, clappers, masks etc.) must bring their own. Only handheld props may be used. Topic:- “ Social Media “का समाज और मन पर दबाव: क्या है बचाव” The teams would be judged on the basis of originality, acting.



P09 MUSY ZEST

Category:- Solo Song Genre/Form:- Semi – Classical Filmy Song Time Limit: - 5 Minutes on and off stage. Theme: - Raag based song on any of these raags. ( Bhairavi, Ashavari, Alahaiya, Bilawal, Jaunpuri) Participants: - 1 + 3 accompanist. Only students can play the Instruments for the Solo performance, to sing with or give backing vocals/ humming to the solo singer. Participating teams may use at the most 3 instruments. Participating teams should bring their own instruments. Participants should perform in school uniform, props and costumes are not permitted. Negative marking will be done for exceeding the time limit.
